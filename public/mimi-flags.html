MiMi Flags - Flag Quiz

Explore, discover, and learn the fascinating world of flags.

Unleash your inner vexillologist with MiMi Flags! Explore the fascinating world of flags and discover hidden connections between their designs. MiMi analyzes flags from around the globe, categorizing them based on shared symbols, colors, and patterns. Dive deep into the world of flag design and uncover surprising similarities. While MiMi Flags doesn't allow you to *create* flags, it's the perfect tool for anyone interested in flag making, providing a rich source of inspiration and design ideas. Explore existing flags categorized by common elements, perfect for sparking your own creative flag designs. Learn about the symbolism and history behind different flag elements, expanding your vexillological knowledge. MiMi Flags is a must-have for flag enthusiasts, history buffs, and anyone curious about the world's flags.
