🎮 HOW TO PLAY?

You can rotate the shapes by sliding your finger up. Tap the screen once to quickly pull the shapes down. Combine the correct number of shapes of the same type. Trigger matches and create space. Achieve a high score with chain combinations

⚡ KEY FEATURES 🧠 Strategy-focused mechanics, different from classic Tetris 🌈 Neon visuals and fluid animations 💥 Chain combo system 🎯 Horizontal, vertical, and diagonal matching 🕹️ Easy to learn, difficult to master

🔥 WHY IS IT ADDICTIVE? Because it's not just reflexes… It's a game of planning, foresight, and perfect timing. Every perfect match is satisfying. Every combo makes you want more. 🎯 WHAT IS YOUR GOAL? To achieve the highest score? Or to create a flawless combo chain?
